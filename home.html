ABOUT

We Improve Overall Health Besides helping with weight loss goals, wellness centers in hospitals can help patients recover from illness or injuries and get healthier. People get a space to get together and bond with others who have similar interests while also helping improve their overall health.

SERVICES

Our center provides a holistic approach to wellness, offering services such as yoga, meditation, nutritional counseling, stress management, and more. One of the key benefits of visiting our wellness center is the personalized care and attention you receive.

VISION

The Health and Wellness Center is committed to delivering the highest quality of health services which include prevention, promotion, and medical care for all students, faculty, and staff. The confidentiality of patient health is of utmost importance in achieving a level of trust and satisfaction. Incorporated into the philosophy of the Health and Wellness Center is the integration of treating the whole person --- mind, body, and spirit.

MISSION

Render invaluable medical services in an atmosphere of respect to the entire campus community. Increase student knowledge by promoting positive healthy lifestyle choices through educational programs and outreach events conducted by the student peer educators under the direction of the wellness coordinator. Maintain prevailing knowledge of evolving medical trends and respond with quality treatment and education.
